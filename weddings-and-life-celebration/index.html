Custom Wedding Ceremonies

My services as a Certified Life-Cycle Celebrant begin with getting to know your love story as a couple. As our connection deepens through the extensive questionnaire that each couple fills out I begin the process of weaving together a customized ceremony that is utterly unique simply because it’s born out of who you are together.

As a Registered Metaphysical Minister in Ontario I offer a special opportunity to help each couple discover what is sacred about their union without involving any beliefs or spiritual preferences of my own, or those of any unwanted institution. Even when couples are unsure of how to bring special presence to an event that helps guests to feel the spiritual or sacred nature of the event my study as a Metaphysical Minister involves apply a wide lens to a wide variety of religions and cultures, giving me the comprehension to support this process regardless of where you’re at.

We are at a beautiful time in history. One in which we are shedding the unnecessary repetition of mindless tradition while simultaneously realizing the importance of ritual and ceremony. This brings us to a place where we are learning to celebrate in new ways while staying inspired by our roots and our ancestry. The end result is learning how to share presence together in such a way as to uplift the meaning and importance of the transitions we each experience throughout life. I invite you to join me in the beautiful process of celebrating presence during a moment in your life that deserves creativity, cultivation and care.
